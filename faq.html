FAQs

What is Odisha Design Week 2021?

A brainchild of the Odisha Design Council headed by our Chairman, Prof. Praresh Choudhury, the Odisha Design Week is an internation design conclave, a five-day festival to celebrate design globally and bridge the gap between the culture-rich state of Odisha and Mordern design practices.

Why attend the Odisha Design Week 2021?

Odisha Design Week 2021 presents to you a platform for experiencing the global spread of design in various sectors of society, understanding Design, and Culture from some of the most celebrated faces of the Design Industry. Enjoy workshops, curated talks, culture sprouting from the soil of Odisha, and network with our speakers in Q&A sessions.

How can you be a part of this first-ever Design Week of Odisha?

One can register for the event on our website www.odishadesignweek.com in the ‘Early Bird’ and ‘Regular’ categories. Registrations are open to both students and professionals at very nominal rates. Register now to get the best discounts in the Early Bird category!

How can I be a part of the workshops?

One can join the link provided on the e-mail post-registration. Workshops shall happen parallelly every day from 10 AM – 12 PM. One will be able to attend only one workshop at a time post-login for the day.

Will I receive a refund should I wish to withdraw from the Event?

The attendees WILL NOT be refunded once they have registered for the event. The amount paid stands valid for the program lineup spread over all five days starting from 11 December 2021 to 15 December 2021.
